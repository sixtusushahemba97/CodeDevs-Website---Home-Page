Guideline Starting as a Developer.

Starting as a Developer or more precisely, UI/UX Design. One is expected to have a decent knowledge and a clear understanding of atleast the three Languages listed bellow to becoming a Frontend(FE) Developer.


With a clear and decent understanding of those Languages, You might then, pickup any Backend Languages and continue from there. Nawadays, almost every Company are more strict on hiring and employing Fullstack(FS) Developers. A Fullstack Developer is a professional Developer that have a decent knowledge of both Frontend(FE) and Backend(BE) development. For one to become a Fullstack(FS) Developer, They must be good at the Client side and Server side.
